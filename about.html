About tradeMark
We are a stock trading resource for beginners.

In phase one, we will share real-time info on your favorite stocks along with news articles to learn more about trading. In the future, we plan to send push notifications when your favorite stocks are at low and high prices. We also plan to provide tutorials, podcasts, and additional learning tools so that you can make the most out of your stock portfolio!
